GeoBel

Winner of the Build for Belarus 2020 hackathon

GeoBel shows real-time locations of protests, medical points, OMON forces and more, on a map of Minsk in Belarus.The app can be quickly reused to support protesters in similar scenarios in other places. The app is open and its aim is to increase the safety of protesters.

Solar Charging Stations

UrbanLab Gdynia 2020 – Pomysły na Miasto
Official Selection

Publicly available solar-powered charging stations, created for residents to charge their electric devices such as mobile phones or electric scooters. The project foresaw the installation of solar powered charging stations in frequently visited places by residents and tourists. The stations will make it possible to charge mobile devices or electric vehicles using renewable energy for the benefit of the urban environment.

WasteHub

UrbanLab Gdynia 2020 – Pomysły na Miasto
Official Selection

Neighborhood workshop. The aim of the project was to decrease ecological footprint of waste, by reusing it to create urban furniture. We planned waste-drop-off points for professional workshops, where people could learn to reshape them into useful objects.
