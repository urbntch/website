products

GeoBel

GeoBel ❤️✊✌

GeoBel shows real-time locations of protests, medical points, OMON forces and more, on a map of Minsk in Belarus.The app can be quickly reused to support protesters in similar scenarios in other places. The app is open and its aim is to increase the safety of protesters. The project is a winner of the Build for Belarus hackathon.

Cities for people

Solving the issue of poor information sharing in the field of orders and delivery in construction industry, tracking construction vehicles as they travel from producers to the aimed construction sites. An asset tracking application where specific vehicles are located on the map and therefore their reach and time of arrival. It attempts to improve delivery system reliability by reducing collisions and inability to unload deliveries at the same time.

Who are we and what we do

We are a team of architects, urbanists and software engineers who integrate their expertise to contribute to a development for a cooler future.

We have particular focus on grassroots level and are in search of programs who tackle vulnerable societies or troubled urban areas. We think the afore-mentioned tools and methodologies should be used in order to provide a better living for people around the globe.

As the world we live in is changing at a fast pace, we thought of uniting our skills and wrapping them through a technological filter in order to adjust to the upcoming changes and tackle the everyday issues on a more expert level.
